In the early 1900s, Korea succeeded in its independence and was able to escape from Japanese colonial policy. The Japanese forces withdrew from Korea quickly because important strongholds had been captured by the resistance forces. The resistance army could succeed in the independence movement thanks to flexible communication and fund management based on smooth contact with each other. The unique method of contact played a major role in securing weapons and receiving base funds safely. In 2022, in celebration of Independence Day, the magazine published four types of magazines as a special project. They will show you what methods of communication existed in each decade and how they gradually evolved.
